Rupert and Ron

  1. Rupert’s full name is Rupert Alexander Lloyd Grint.
  2. Amazingly, he owns an ice cream truck!
  3. Similar to his large family in Harry Potter, Rupert is the eldest of five children.
  4. Rupert joined a local theatre school and was cast as a fish in Noah’s Ark and a donkey in another play.
  5. Like his co-star Emma Watson, Rupert had never acted professionally before Harry Potter.
  6. Ronald’s full name is Ronald Bilius Weasley.
  7. Although not intended at the start, Ron is inspired by J.K Rowling’s best friend.
  8. Ron’s insecurities come from a number of factors, those being: he is not an excellent Quidditch player nor a noteworthy study, he was not the daughter his mother always dreamt of and often although not purposefully he is overshadowed by Harry’s fame.
  9. Before Ron begins his adult life working as an Auror in the Ministry and Magic, he joins Fred and George at Weasleys’ Wizard Wheezes, a shop which becomes a huge success.
  10. In the final instalment Ron and Hermione are married with two children called Rose and Hugo.
